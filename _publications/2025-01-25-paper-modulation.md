title: "Turbulence modulation in particle-laden channel flow: the particle inertial effects"
collection: publications
permalink: /publication/2025-01-25-paper-modulation
excerpt: "Four-way coupled PP-DNS reveals that increasing particle inertia shifts the flow from drag enhancement to reduction before asymptotically recovering the single-phase state. In the statistical sense, competition between particle-induced extra transport and extra dissipation at various levels are identified as the drivers of turbulence modulation, leveraging a comprehensive two-phase statistical description derived in this work. The particle inertial effects are clarified by examining the limit behaviors (St to 0 or ∞) analytically."
