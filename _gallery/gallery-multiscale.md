excerpt: "Turbulent flows are highly dynamic, irregular, and seemingly chaotic, featured by swirling eddies and coherent structures across a vast range of spatiotemporal scales. These complex motions generally emerge at high Reynolds numbers. Here, we present an instantaneous snapshot of a turbulent channel flow at a friction Reynolds number of 2000, highlighting the intricate vortical structures using the Q-criterion. The smaller panels illustrate the evolution of near-wall streaky patterns as the Reynolds number increases from 300 to 750 and finally to 2000, revealing the progressive intensification of turbulence. <br/><img src='/images/gallery-re.jpg'>"
